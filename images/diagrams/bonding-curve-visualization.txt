Mathematical Formula:
===================

Price = InitialPrice × (CurrentSupply ÷ InitialSupply)^Exponent

Parameters:
==========
• Initial Price: 0.0001 ETH
• Initial Supply: 1,000,000 tokens
• Exponent: 1.5 (controls curve steepness)
• Max Supply: 800,000,000 tokens

Curve Behavior:
==============

Linear Curve (Exponent = 1.0):
- Steady, predictable price increase
- Good for stable, long-term projects
- Lower risk, slower growth

Exponential Curve (Exponent = 1.5):
- Accelerated price growth
- Higher potential returns
- Increased volatility

Custom Curve (Exponent = variable):
- Tailored to project needs
- Flexible tokenomics
- Advanced use cases
