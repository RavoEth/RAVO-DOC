Detailed Fee Breakdown:
======================

1. TRANSACTION FEE COLLECTION
   ├── Input: User pays 1% platform fee
   ├── Processing: Fee deducted automatically
   └── Security: Reentrancy protection applied

2. FEE ALLOCATION ALGORITHM
   ├── Creator: 50% (fixed)
   │   ├── Immediate distribution for project funding
   │   ├── Reserve fund for emergencies
   │   └── Development fund for growth
   │
   ├── Platform: 25% (fixed)
   │   ├── Operations: Infrastructure costs
   │   ├── Development: Product improvement
   │   ├── Security: Audits and monitoring
   │   └── Marketing: User acquisition
   │
   └── Ecosystem: 25% (fixed)
       ├── Buyback: Token value enhancement
       ├── Partnerships: Strategic collaborations
       └── Community: Rewards and incentives

3. DISTRIBUTION MECHANISM
   ├── Timing: Every 2 hours or when threshold reached
   ├── Method: Direct ETH transfers to beneficiary addresses
   ├── Security: Multi-signature controls for large distributions
   └── Transparency: All distributions logged on-chain

4. BENEFITS FOR EACH PARTY
   ├── Creators: Passive income from trading activity
   ├── Platform: Sustainable funding for growth
   ├── Traders: Low fees, high liquidity
   └── Ecosystem: Self-sustaining token economy
